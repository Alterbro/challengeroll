About

This is randomizer for Saturday challenge(but you try it whenever you wish). The logic is simple:
>Roll your party
>Post what you get
>Setup party
>Try to beat the free quest "Dewar" or "Infinitude Territory"
>Post result
Read the rules for details and for more challenging aproach. P.S Design is shit, but at least it is usable.

Rules

  1. Free Quest: "Dewar" or "Infinitude Territory", unless you still didn't clear LB4.
  2. Having 4-5* servant in party is not allowed. Unless you can't make party full of 1-3 star Servants, then you can do randomizer with 4* servants.
  3. Kaleido and BG are not allowed.
  4. Only one MLB CE with 50% or more NP Gauge in the party.
  5. You are not allowed to use Support Servant. Use Mash lvl 1, or ask your friend to pick somebody lvl 1.
  6. Plugsuit prohibited.
  7. Mash is not allowed, unless it's Support Servant with very low lvl.
  8. Your servants have to be MAX ascension. (But feel free to try without MAX ascension).
  9. Level of skillset doesn't matter, as well as the final level of the servant. Grailed servants allowed.
  10. Post roll screen with party setup in the thread, play it, then post result(even fail).
  11. Rerolls are allowed if you got Mash for some unfixed reason, or if you got Servants that you don't have.
  12. If you are rolling 4/5 Star Servant gacha, and you don't have 4/5* Servant that you rolled, reroll only for another 4/5 Start Sevant without changing other servants in your party.
  13. Rules can be edited in future to make some moments more clearer/difficult.

Good luck, /alter/!
