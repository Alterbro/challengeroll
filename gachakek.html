About

This is randomizer for Saturday challenge(but you can do it whenever you wish). The logic is simple:
>Roll your party
>Post what you get
>Setup party
>Try to beat the free quest "Dewar" or "Infinitude Territory"
>Post result
See rules for details and more challenging aproach. P.S Design is shit, but at least it is usable.

Rules

  1. Free Quest: "Dewar" or "Infinitude Territory", unless you still didn't clear LB4.
  2. Having 4-5* servant in party is not allowed. Unless you can't make it full 1-3 star Servants, then you can do randomizer with 4* servants.
  3. Kaleido, BG is not allowed.
  4. Only one MLB CE with 50% or more NP Gauge in party.
  5. You are not allowed to use Support Servant. Use Mash lvl 1, or ask friend about picking somebody lvl 1 for some time.
  6. Plugsuit prohibitaited.
  7. Mash is not allowed, unless it's Support Servant with very low lvl.
  8. Your servants have to be MAX ascension. (But feel free to try without MAX ascension).
  9. Level of skillset doen't matter, as well as final level of servant.
  10. Post roll screen with party presetup in thread, play it, then post result(even fail).
  11. Rerolls are allowed if you get Mash in it for some unfixed reason.
  12. Rules can be edited in future to make some moments more clearer/difficult.

Good luck, /alter/!
